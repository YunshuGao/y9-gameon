L19 · Week 10 ⏱ 75 minutes 🎯 IND5-2 · IND5-5 · IND5-9 📂 Phase 4 — Polish, Twist & Showcase

Scoring, play button, and difficulty

Learning Intentions

  • I can display a live score using pygame.font
  • I can add a Play button to start/restart
  • I can make the game get harder as you score higher

Success Criteria

  • My score shows in the corner while playing
  • There's a Play button that starts the game
  • The aliens speed up over time
5 min

Do Now — Error of the Day

Think about your favourite arcade game (any from Term 1). What kept you playing? Likely: the score going up.

10 min

I Do — Live-coded demo

Live-code a Scoreboard class that uses pygame.font.SysFont. Show self.font.render(f"Score: {stats.score}", True, text_color, bg_color). Show the Button class: a coloured rect with centred text. Click detection via button.rect.collidepoint().

15 min

We Do — Guided build

Integrate Scoreboard: update score on collision, call sb.show_score() each frame. Integrate Button: show on game start; hide once clicked; show again on game over.

35 min

You Do — Your turn

Tune difficulty: in _check_bullet_alien_collisions, when fleet is cleared, call self.settings.increase_speed() which multiplies ship_speed, bullet_speed, alien_speed by 1.1. Playtest — does it feel right?

Support

Use scoring_starter.py with Scoreboard pre-written.
