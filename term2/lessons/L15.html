Lesson 15: Design + Pitch Your Own 2D Game

Learning Intentions

  • Choose a genre, design your own 2D game, get teacher approval.

Success Criteria

  • I've picked a genre from the approved list (or pitched my own).
  • I've written a 1-page Game Design Document.
  • Teacher has approved my concept.

📍 Do Now — Error of the Day

Browse your phone for 2 minutes. What 2D mobile game would you want to copy the idea of? Why?

👁 I Do — Teacher Demo

Walk through game design fundamentals: core mechanic, goal, challenge, feedback loop. Show 4 example GDDs (shooter, platformer, maze, puzzle).

🤝 We Do — Build Together

Class fills in the first row of a GDD template together — we pick a simple sample game and brainstorm its GDD.

🚀 You Do — Your Turn

Fill in YOUR GDD. Pick your genre. Pitch to Ms Gao in a 2-min slot. Get approval + 2 pieces of feedback.

  • Pick genre: shooter, platformer, maze, endless runner, puzzle, racing, pong/breakout, or pitch your own
  • Complete GDD template: concept, core mechanic, controls, win/lose, art style
  • Pitch your GDD to teacher (2 minutes)
  • Receive teacher approval + 2 pieces of feedback
