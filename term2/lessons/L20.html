Lesson 20: Custom Sprite Art + Reskin Prep

Learning Intentions

  • Replace the default ship + alien sprites with your own pixel art.
  • Preview how a full reskin will look.

Success Criteria

  • I've drawn a custom 32×48 ship sprite in piskelapp.com.
  • I've drawn at least 1 custom alien sprite.
  • My sprites load and display without errors.

📍 Do Now — Error of the Day

Open piskelapp.com (no login). Create a new 32×48 sprite. Draw one coloured blob. Export as PNG. Now you've made pixel art.

👁 I Do — Teacher Demo

Projector tour of piskelapp.com — palette, pencil, bucket, export. Live-draw a simple 48×48 alien while you watch (5 min).

🤝 We Do — Build Together

Drop the exported file into your assets/ folder. Update the pygame.image.load path in Ship.__init__. Run the game — your sprite appears.

🚀 You Do — Your Turn

Draw YOUR ship (32×48) + at least ONE custom alien (48×48). Export + swap in. Your game now visibly YOURS. Arcade quests below are general art + reskin prep.

  • Go to piskelapp.com (free, no login)
  • Draw a 32×48 ship sprite (or 48×48 for a spaceship)
  • Export as PNG or BMP to /assets/
  • Change Ship.__init__ path: pygame.image.load('assets/my_ship.bmp')
  • Do the same for at least 1 alien sprite
  • Save + playtest
