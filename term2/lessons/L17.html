L17 · Week 9 ⏱ 75 minutes 🎯 IND5-2 · IND5-3 · IND5-5 📂 Phase 3 — Alien Invasion Mechanics

Shooting aliens — collision detection

Learning Intentions

  • I can use pygame.sprite.groupcollide() to detect bullet-alien collisions
  • I can remove destroyed aliens and bullets
  • I can respawn the fleet when all aliens are gone

Success Criteria

  • Hitting an alien with a bullet removes both
  • When the fleet is empty, a new fleet appears
  • My game feels satisfying when you hit an alien
5 min

Do Now — Error of the Day

Your ship has a rect. Your bullet has a rect. Your alien has a rect. How would Python know if a bullet hit an alien? (pygame.Rect.colliderect — or the Sprite equivalent.)

10 min

I Do — Live-coded demo

Live-code collision: 

def _check_bullet_alien_collisions(self):
    collisions = pygame.sprite.groupcollide(
        self.bullets, self.aliens, True, True)
    if not self.aliens:
        self.bullets.empty()
        self._create_fleet()
Explain True, True — delete bullet AND alien on collision. Could be False for piercing bullets.

15 min

We Do — Guided build

Integrate collision check into main loop. Test: fire at aliens and they disappear. Fire at all aliens — the fleet respawns. Smile.

35 min

You Do — Your turn
