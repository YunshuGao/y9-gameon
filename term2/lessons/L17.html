Lesson 17: Sound Effects + Explosion Particles

Learning Intentions

  • Add juice — SFX on firing, explosions on alien hits, particle burst visuals.
  • Game feel lesson.

Success Criteria

  • Firing a bullet plays a sound.
  • Hitting an alien plays an explosion sound + shows a particle burst.
  • I can explain the 'game feel' concept.

📍 Do Now — Error of the Day

Play any arcade game on your phone for 60 seconds with the sound OFF. Then play for 60 seconds with the sound ON. How different does it feel?

👁 I Do — Teacher Demo

Live-code pygame.mixer.init(), loading Sound('shoot.wav'), calling .play(). Show volume control via .set_volume().

🤝 We Do — Build Together

Add shoot sfx + explode sfx to the game together. Build the Particle class — position, velocity, gravity, life, draw as a circle.

🚀 You Do — Your Turn

Spawn 12 particles at each alien-death position. Tune the colour + life + gravity until it looks right. Arcade quests below are general Pygame + feedback patterns.

  • Download 2 free SFX from freesound.org (shoot + explosion)
  • pygame.mixer.init() in Game.__init__
  • self.shoot_sfx = pygame.mixer.Sound('sfx/shoot.wav')
  • Call self.shoot_sfx.play() on bullet fire
  • Build Particle class (x, y, vx, vy, life). Draw as small circles.
  • Spawn 12 particles at alien collision point
