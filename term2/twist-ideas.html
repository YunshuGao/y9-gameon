🌀 Your Twist Ideas

Every student must add ONE original feature to their Alien Invasion. Pick from below — or invent your own and get it approved in Lesson 19.

Why this matters

Your Twist is worth /5 marks and is what makes YOUR game YOUR game. It's also what your evaluation is about. Pick something challenging but finishable in one 75-minute lesson.

🛡️ Power-Ups · Difficulty: Medium

Add a shield, rapid-fire, or slow-time pickup that drops occasionally from destroyed aliens.

How to start: Create a PowerUp class inheriting from Sprite. Spawn randomly from collision events. Check ship-powerup collision. Apply a timed effect (self.shield_active = True for 5 seconds).

👾 Boss Alien · Difficulty: Hard

One huge alien that takes multiple hits and fires back at you.

How to start: BossAlien class with hp attribute. Override take_damage to decrement hp. Don't kill until hp=0. Add its own Bullet class that fires at the ship.

🎵 Sound Effects & Music · Difficulty: Easy

Add a laser sound when firing, an explosion when hitting an alien, and background music.

How to start: Use pygame.mixer. Download free sounds from freesound.org. pygame.mixer.Sound(file).play().

🌟 Multiple Levels · Difficulty: Medium

After clearing one fleet, go to Level 2 — different alien arrangement, faster speed, new background.

How to start: Add a level attribute to GameStats. In _check_bullet_alien_collisions when fleet empty: increment level, call settings.increase_speed(), redraw background from a list of images.

🎨 Custom Ship & Alien Skins · Difficulty: Easy

Draw your own pixel art for ship + alien using Piskel (free, browser-based).

How to start: Visit piskelapp.com. Draw 32x32 sprites. Export as PNG. Save in your game folder. Replace ship.bmp and alien.bmp in pygame.image.load calls.

👥 Two-Player Local Co-op · Difficulty: Very Hard

Player 1 uses arrows, Player 2 uses WASD — two ships firing at the same fleet.

How to start: Two Ship instances (ship1, ship2). Handle separate key events. Detect collisions for each. Share the alien fleet.

🔀 Procedural Alien Patterns · Difficulty: Hard

Each level generates a different alien layout — V-shape, diamond, spiral, randomness.

How to start: In _create_fleet, use nested loops with conditions: e.g. if row + col == 5: add alien for diagonal. Switch pattern based on stats.level % 4.

🏆 High-Score Leaderboard · Difficulty: Medium

Save the top 5 scores with player initials to a file; show them on the game-over screen.

How to start: Save to highscores.txt using Python's open() with 'w'. Load with open() 'r'. Sort scores list descending, take top 5. Ask player for initials on game over.

💥 Particle Explosion · Difficulty: Medium

When an alien dies, spawn 20 tiny rectangles that fly outward and fade.

How to start: Particle class with position, velocity, life. On alien death, spawn 20 Particles at alien position with random velocities. Update each frame; remove when life <= 0.

🎯 Your Own Idea · Difficulty: You decide

Propose something not on this list to your teacher in Lesson 19.

How to start: Write up: what does it do? what code will you need? what's the hardest part? Teacher approves before Lesson 20.
