💡
Last-minute debugging tips:
  • NullReferenceException? — Check Inspector: is the field assigned? Drag the object in.
  • Scene won't load? — File → Build Settings → Add Open Scenes for ALL scenes.
  • Player falls through ground? — Check both objects have Colliders. Ground should NOT have isTrigger.
  • Score not resetting? — Reset your static variables in Start() or on scene load.
  • Sound not playing? — Check AudioSource component exists and AudioClip is assigned.
