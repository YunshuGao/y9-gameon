1 What Is Game Design?

Game design means planning how a game works. It is not drawing. It is not coding. It is PLANNING.
Every game has a core mechanic — the main thing the player does over and over again.
Examples of core mechanics:
• Jump — Mario
• Build — Minecraft
• Shoot — Fortnite
• Collect — Pokemon
A Genre is the type of game. Examples: Platformer, Puzzle, RPG, Shooter, Simulation, Adventure.

Key Vocabulary

Game Designer
The person who plans how a game works.
Core Mechanic
The main action the player does most in the game.
Genre
The type/category of the game (e.g. Platformer, RPG).
Flow
When a game is not too easy and not too hard — it feels just right.
What is your favourite game? What do you DO most in it? That’s the core mechanic!

4 Create a Mood Board in Canva

A mood board is a collection of pictures that show the style and feeling of your game — characters, environments, colours, and objects.
Open Canva and log in to your account. You should see the Canva home page.
Click “Create a design” → choose Custom size → type 1080 x 1080 px → click Create new design. A square blank canvas opens.
Click “Elements” on the left sidebar. Search for images that match your game — try searching for your characters, environments, colours, and objects. You will see lots of images, graphics, and stickers.
Drag 6–10 images onto your canvas. Arrange them so they look good together. Resize and move them to fill the canvas. Your canvas shows images that represent the style of your game.
Add a text box with your game title at the top. Make it big and bold. Your mood board has a title and 6–10 images.
Rename your design: click the name at the top and type “L09 - Game Mood Board - [Your Name]”. The design is saved with the correct name.
Not sure what to search for? Think about: What does the world of your game look like? What colours? What characters?
