1 Showcase Day!

This is the day you show off all your hard work!

Today is a celebration. You will present your game design portfolio to your classmates, and you'll get to see (and play!) the Unity games that other students built.

Key Words

Showcase
An event where people present their work
Pitch
A short speech explaining your project to someone
Gallery Walk
When everyone walks around looking at each other's work
I am ready to present!

2 Prepare Your Presentation (10 min)

Your Pitch: “My game is called ___. It is a ___ game where you ___. The coolest thing about my game is ___.”
Nervous? That's totally normal! Everyone feels nervous before presenting. Take a deep breath. You know your game better than anyone!

3 Present Your Portfolio (40 min)

Gallery Walk format: Everyone opens their portfolio on their screen.

Good compliments: “I really like your character design because...” “Your level looks fun because...” “The colours you chose are...”
Good questions: “How did you come up with that idea?” “What was the hardest part?” “What would you add if you had more time?”
Visit at least 4 classmates' work. Leave a kind comment or sticky note for each one.

You Did It!

You completed an entire game design course. You learned about game genres, characters, levels, animation, sound, UI design, and professional game design documents. Be proud of what you achieved!
